Welcome to Ontario's Jumping Spider Community

Ontario is home to an incredible diversity of jumping spiders (Salticidae), each with unique behaviors and adaptations. These tiny acrobats of the arachnid world captivate with their intelligence, vibrant colors, and remarkable hunting skills.

What makes jumping spiders special? Their exceptional vision (with eyes that can see in nearly 360 degrees), complex courtship dances, and ability to leap up to 50 times their body length set them apart from other spiders.

Whether you're a curious beginner or an experienced arachnophile, our resources will help you:

  • Identify local species through our visual guide
  • Understand their ecological importance
  • Learn safe observation techniques
  • Contribute to citizen science projects
